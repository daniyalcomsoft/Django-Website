Who We Are

Tetra Tech was established in 2003 with one window service concept. Today, Tetra Tech is leading industrial services company covering Middle East , African & Asian countries that specifically includes Pakistan and having plans to expand the horizons in Afghanistan and Iran region in the field of Supply of materials , installation, commissioning, testing and startup operations of small, medium and large scale dairy, beverages, water, food, confectionery, cosmetic and pharmaceutical industries.

Our Goal

Tetra Tech’s vision firmly believes in quality and safety with high regard given to implementing the following aspects:
  • Strict adherence to safety procedures during Installation and Operation.
  • Inducting Traceability and Operational Accountability in process design.
  • Quality certification of materials used according to International Standards.
  • Expert craftsmanship in compliance with the specific demands of industry.
  • Collaboration with only ISO Certified world class companies.
  • Addressing good manufacturing practices, design qualifications, installation qualifications, operation qualifications and process qualifications required by the industry.
  • Implementing Safety Hazard Control procedures according to HACCP.
  • Regular detailed training of staff on safety and quality.
  • Hiring Certified Welding and Fabricating staff, who maintain a high level of hygiene during installation.

Tetra Tech is committed to provide customized, state of art and cost effective products and engineering services to its valued customers.
