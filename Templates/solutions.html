Plant Engineering

Tetra Tech provides multi-disciplined engineering and project management services including conceptual engineering, front-end engineering, detailed engineering and design, project management controls and estimating, with procurement, construction, turnaround, commissioning and start-up support services as needed.

Further Services

No matter whether you’re a newcomer or a sectoral expert – we have the specialists who can provide the comprehensively can-do support you need for planning a new facility in terms of not only actual production, but also filling, packaging, Automation Services & Material. In closely responsive consultation with you, from a multiplicity of possible options, we select the technologically and commercially most effective variant, handle the design and planning work, and bring the project to a successful conclusion at your chosen location. Tetra Tech is not only the consultant here, but also takes responsibility for executing the entire project.

Beyond the planning phase, if you are planning an expansion of your production facility we have capability to support you in expanding your production plants. We steer your project from the ground-breaking ceremony all the way through to successful commissioning, and also during the product life-cycle. The experience gained from numerous complete factories built underpins our corporate planning capabilities, and guarantees that the planning work involved is always based on the very latest state of the art.

Your logistics are coming up against their capacity limits, your overall plant capabilities are no longer satisfactory, your processes are time- and cost-intensive, you lack the requisite flexibility, or you’re planning to buy new machines or lines? Then we will come to you, discuss your wishes on the spot, analyze the as-is situation, and acquire the data required for meaningful consultancy. With new data, we formulate possible alternatives, with the associated costings, for optimizing your processes in terms of both economy and ecology. We advise you on how you can carry out the proposed changes while retaining your delivery capabilities, and plan the appropriate implementation concepts. Checking the planned measures against the relevant key statistics is part of the job package, of course
